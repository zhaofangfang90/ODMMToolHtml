1. Value Measurement
Do you assess the value which your analytics deliver to the enterprise? How do you assign causality between analytic modelling and actual business results?
Best
Each analytics initiative is measured against formal ROI criteria. Total investment, ongoing costs, and headcount are mapped against an absolute return-on-investment figure that has been determined through causal means. Business results are predicted, scientifically isolated, and tested for significance with respect to predicted uncertainty.
